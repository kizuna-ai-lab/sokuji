Last Updated: June 3, 2025

Introduction

Sokuji Extension ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our browser extension and desktop application. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the extension.

We NEVER collect, store, or transmit:

  • Audio recordings or voice content
  • Translation text or conversation content
  • Personal information (names, emails, addresses)
  • Location data or IP addresses
  • Any sensitive or personally identifiable information

Information We Collect

User-Provided Information

  • OpenAI API Key: You provide your own OpenAI API key which is stored locally on your device.
  • Audio Content: The extension processes audio from your microphone when you actively use the interpretation feature. This audio is processed in real-time and is not stored.
  • Configuration Settings: Your preferences for language models, voice settings, and other configuration options.

Analytics Data (Optional)

With your explicit consent, we collect anonymous usage analytics to improve Sokuji:

  • App Usage Patterns: Which features you use and how often
  • Performance Metrics: App startup time, translation latency, and error rates
  • Device Information: Operating system, device type (anonymized)
  • Language Preferences: Source and target language selections
  • Settings Changes: When you modify app settings (not the content of settings)

You can opt-out of analytics at any time through the app settings.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Technical Data: Basic usage statistics to improve the extension's functionality (only with consent).
  • Extension State: Information about the extension's current state (active/inactive) on specific tabs.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in the following ways:

  • To provide real-time language interpretation services
  • To create and manage virtual audio devices for communication applications
  • To save your preferences and settings between sessions
  • To improve and optimize the extension's performance (with your consent)
  • To understand user behavior and improve the user experience (with your consent)
  • To identify and fix bugs and performance issues (with your consent)

Analytics and Tracking

PostHog Analytics

We use PostHog, a privacy-focused analytics platform, to understand how users interact with Sokuji. This helps us improve the application and fix issues.

Your Control Over Analytics

  • Explicit Consent Required: Analytics are only enabled after you explicitly consent
  • Easy Opt-Out: You can disable analytics at any time in the app settings
  • Granular Control: Choose what types of data you're comfortable sharing
  • GDPR Compliant: Full compliance with European privacy regulations

What Analytics Data Includes

  • Feature usage frequency and patterns
  • App performance metrics (startup time, memory usage)
  • Error reports and crash information (anonymized)
  • Language pair preferences and usage statistics
  • Device type and operating system (anonymized)

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • All user data, including your OpenAI API key and configuration settings, is stored locally on your device using the browser's secure storage mechanisms.
  • No user data is stored on our servers.
  • Analytics consent preferences are stored locally on your device.

Data Transmission

  • Audio data is transmitted directly from your browser to OpenAI's servers using your provided API key.
  • Analytics data (if consented) is transmitted to PostHog's secure servers.
  • All data transmission occurs over secure HTTPS connections.
  • No audio or translation content is ever transmitted to our analytics servers.

Third-Party Services

OpenAI

  • When you use the interpretation feature, audio data is sent to OpenAI's servers for processing.
  • This transmission is governed by OpenAI's Privacy Policy.
  • Your use of OpenAI's services through our extension is subject to OpenAI's terms of service.

PostHog Analytics (Optional)

  • If you consent to analytics, anonymous usage data is sent to PostHog for analysis.
  • PostHog is a privacy-focused analytics platform with strong data protection measures.
  • PostHog's data handling is governed by their Privacy Policy.
  • You can opt-out of PostHog analytics at any time through the app settings.

Data Retention

  • Your configuration data is retained locally on your device until you uninstall the extension or clear your browser data.
  • Audio data is processed in real-time and is not persistently stored by the extension.
  • Analytics data (if consented) is retained by PostHog according to their retention policy (typically 7 years, configurable).
  • OpenAI may retain data according to their privacy policy.
  • You can request deletion of your analytics data by contacting us.

User Rights and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your information stored locally by the extension
  • Opt-out of analytics tracking at any time through the app settings
  • Request deletion of any analytics data we may have collected
  • Be informed of how your data is being used
  • Withdraw consent for analytics tracking without affecting core app functionality
  • Export your local settings and preferences
